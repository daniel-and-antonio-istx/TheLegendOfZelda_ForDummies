Two variations of a useful tool giving Link super strength. First appearing in The Legend of Zelda, it was used simply for pushing boulders that open shortcuts around the land of Hyrule. In The Adventure of Link, it was called the Handy Glove, and was used for breaking blocks with the Sword.
