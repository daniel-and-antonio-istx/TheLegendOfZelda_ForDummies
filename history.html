• Four Swords

    Released: 2nd December, 2002

    Vaati, a wind mage appears in Hyrule to abduct girls. A young boy bravely enters Vaati's cave to save the girls but is split into 4. Later on, Zelda and Link investigate the Shrine where Zelda is kidnapped. Link uses the power of the 4 swords to save Zelda from Vaati.

  • Wind Waker

    Released: 24th March, 2003

    Hyrule has been completely flooded leaving only 49 islands. Link becomes old enough to wear the hero's clothes until pirates and birds attack leading to the realisation that Tetra is a pirate captain. Link's sister is kidnapped. Link will continue and soon learn slowly about Ganon and stop him.

  • Four Swords Adventures

    Released: 14th May 2001

    One of the first LOZ games to incorporate multiplayer in its main story. Similar to Four Swords, Zelda has Link investigate the Four Sword of legend but is sealed by Dark Link. Link will proceed and save the six maidens to the Palace of winds to fight Vaati.

  • Minish Cap

    Released: 10th January, 2005

    A tournament was held to which a strange man named Vaati won with ease. Zelda had to present him the prize but he was after the Light Force. The protagonist, Link, must defeat Vaati to save Humanity from the possible destruction he would've caused.

Hyrule has been flooded and Link with Tetra will search for the Ghost ship. Upon finding it, Tetra explores it but is kidnapped. Link is helpless and  continues to save Tetra from the Ghost Pirates.

Nintendo/Super Nintendo Entertainment System

  • The Legend of Zelda

    Released: 1986

    Set in Hyrule, a boy named Link must collect 8 fragments of the Triforce of Wisdom to save Princess Zelda from Ganon. Viewing from a birds eye view, the player must go through dungeons and explore the world.

  • The Adventure of Link

    Release: 14th, January 1987

    Link must save Princess Zelda again after she has been afflicted with a sleeping spell. New features like a magic meter was introduced leading to this games financial and critical success.

  • A Link to the Past

    Release: 21st, November 1991

    Link must save Hyrule, defeat Ganon and rescue maidens related to the Sages. With a birds eye view and  the Master Sword, this game was a critical success.

 

Game Boy/Cube/Boy Advance

  • Link's Awakening

    Released: 6th June 1993

    Without Hyrule, Zelda or the Triforce, Link explores Koholint island to find 8 musical instruments to awaken the Wind Fish and escape the island.

     

  • Ocarina of Time

    Release: 21st November, 1998

    A classic well loved Zelda game of third person gameplay in 3D. Link must stop Ganon of the Gerudo's from retrieving the Triforce. This game introduced Target Locking.

  • Majora's Mask

    Release: 27th, April 2000

    With the new concept of masks and a repeated 3 day cycle. With a complex story and 4 temples to stop a moon crashing to Termina.

  • Oracle of Ages

    Released: 27th February, 2001

    Link, Sent to Labrynna due to the Triforce, stops Veran from possessing the Oracle of Ages. Link continues to retrieve the Essence of time and uses the harp to travel through time and find these essences to showdown Veran.

  • Oracle of Seasons

    Released: 14th May 2001

    Similar to Oracle of Ages, Link is sent to Holodrum. Link must restore order and save Din after the Temple of Season falls. Link must fine the Essence of Nature to showdown the villain Onox.

 

Wii / Wii U / Nintendo Switch

  • Twilight Princess

    Released: 19th November, 2006

    A more realistic mature Zelda where Link goes through different forms with Midna as his partner. Together, they must rid Hyrule of the fused shadows led by Zant and Ganon leading to an epic battle on horse back between Ganon and Link.

  • Skyward Sword

    Released: 20th Novmeber, 2011

    Earlier in the Zelda timeline, Link attends an annual ceremony but is told by Zelda that Loftwing is missing. Going on a search for Loftwing, Zelda goes missing and Link must retrieve the Triforce and Sacred Flames to fix the seal containing evil.

  • Breath of the Wild

    Release: 3rd March, 2017

    As an open world game, it was one of the first games for the Nintendo Switch. Based on the first entry in the series, Breath of the Wild places a very large focus on exploration.
