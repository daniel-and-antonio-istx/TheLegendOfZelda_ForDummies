• Hylian

    The master race. Hylians appear to be humans, except with pointy ears, which are supposedly for hearing the voice of the goddess Hylia.

  • Deku

    A race of small, tree-like creatures. Some are aggressive and shoot rocks, while others try to sell items instead.

  • Goron

    Rock-eating creatures that generally live in mountain caves. Usually shown to be friendly.

  • Zora

    Zoras live in lakes, oceans, and other bodies of water. In some games, they are monsters that spit fireballs at Link. In others, though, they are shown as graceful, anthropomorphic fish.

  • Kokiri

    Similar in appearance to Hylians, except Kokiri never grow up, instead remaining as children, watched over by the Great Deku Tree.

  • Gerudo

    A race of desert-dwelling women. One male Gerudo is born every 100 years, and Ganondorf is the most recent Gerudo male.

  • Rito

    Bird-like people with beaks and wings. In The Wind Waker, their guardian is a dragon named Valoo.

  • Korok

    Small, wooden creatures that wear leaf masks obscuring their faces. Their main habit is planting seeds, and they can use leaves as propellers to fly around.
